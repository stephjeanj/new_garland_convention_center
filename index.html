Frequently Asked Questions

What is your venue capacity?

Our venue can comfortably accommodate up to 800 guests.

Can I decorate the venue myself?

Absolutely! We encourage you to decorate the space to fit your vision. In fact, many clients love to come in to set everything up just the way they want it.

What's included in the venue rental?

When you rent our venue, you'll receive tables, chairs, basic table linens, and we'll handle the setup and cleanup for you. You'll also have access to a bridal suite and parking for your guests.

How far in advance should I book?

It's best to book your event as early as you can! Popular dates can fill up several months in advance, so reaching out early helps ensure you get the date you want.

Is there parking available for my guests?

Yes, we provide parking for all your guests. This makes it easy for everyone to come and enjoy your special event without worrying about where to park.
